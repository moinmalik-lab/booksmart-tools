BookSmartTools is a free collection of calculators for small business owners, bookkeepers, freelancers, and anyone dealing with payroll, taxes, loans, or business finances.

Every tool runs entirely in your browser — nothing is sent to a server. Tax rates are pulled from IRS publications and updated each January. If you find an error, let us know and we'll fix it.

  • ✓
    Rates sourced from the IRS — Federal brackets, FICA, SS wage base ($184,500 for 2026), and all state rates updated every January.
  • ✓
    Your data stays on your device — All calculations happen locally in your browser. No account, no cloud, no tracking.
  • ✓
    Always verify with a professional — These tools are for estimation and planning. For tax filing, consult a CPA or licensed advisor.
