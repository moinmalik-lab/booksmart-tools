Late Payment Tips for US Small Businesses
TIP 01
Always state your late fee policy clearly on every invoice — ideally "1.5% per month on overdue balances" — before work begins.
TIP 02
Most US states allow up to 18% APR (1.5%/month). Check your state's usury laws before charging higher rates.
TIP 03
Send an automated reminder at Net-30, Net-45, and Net-60 to reduce the number of invoices that go to collections.
