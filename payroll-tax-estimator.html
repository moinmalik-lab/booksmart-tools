What is FUTA?

Federal Unemployment Tax Act. Employers pay 6% on the first $7,000 of each employee's wages, but receive a 5.4% credit if state unemployment taxes are paid on time — making the effective rate 0.6%.

Employer vs Employee Taxes

FICA taxes are split equally between employer and employee. FUTA and SUTA are employer-only costs. The employee never sees these on their paycheck — they're an extra cost on top of salary.
