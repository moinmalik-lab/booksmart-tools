About

Built by a bookkeeper, for bookkeepers

I'm a professional bookkeeper working remotely with US small business clients. Every tool on this site started as something I needed for my own work.

I got tired of doing late fee math in Excel, estimating payroll taxes from memory, and sending clients to complicated IRS worksheets. So I built simpler tools — and decided to share them.

Everything here is free. No account required. If a tool saves you 20 minutes, that's the win.
