Meals Tax Rates — All 50 States (2026)

Prepared food and restaurant meals tax rates. Most states apply their standard sales tax rate to restaurant food. Some states have a separate higher meals tax rate. Local meals taxes can add significant additional amounts on top of the state rate — especially in Virginia, Massachusetts, South Carolina, and other states that authorize local meals taxes. Source: State revenue departments, Tax Foundation 2026, RestaurantCPAs.com.
